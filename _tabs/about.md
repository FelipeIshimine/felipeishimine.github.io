Game Developer and Programmer from Argentina. I've been making games since 2015 as a hobby, and professionally since 2019. I love building tight game feel, writing clean systems, and shipping things people can actually play.

My work spans mobile, web, PC, and Steam — from solo game jam entries to commercial titles published on Poki and the Unity Asset Store.

- **Gameplay:** Physics systems, platformers, turn-based combat, AI, pathfinding (A*)
