# Description
![Gameplay-1](/assets/images/kunai-screenshot-1.png){: .right}
Fast phased action platformer.
Entry for the SpeedRun Jam.

You play as a little Red Panda ninja that has to infiltrate the evil robots base. You play by swiping/DragAndDrop. 
Swiping while grounded will make you Jump. Swiping on the air, will throw a Kunai. Kunai bounces on hard surfaces, and teleports the player when it stucks to soft surfaces. Player and also Wall Jump and Slide, depending on how they jump. 

![Gameplay-3](/assets/images/kunai-screenshot-2.png){: .right}
Ilumination is 2D dynamic with a Lut table to replace colors based on brightness
