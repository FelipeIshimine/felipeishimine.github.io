## Description

Exploration + turn-based combat. You play as an Alchemist working for the Dungeon Classification Agency (West Division), tasked with registering every entity inside a dungeon before your shift ends.

Made in 2 weeks for **Pirate Software Game Jam 15** with the theme *"Alchemy and Shadows"*.
