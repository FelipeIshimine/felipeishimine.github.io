Grass Cutter is a simulation game where you trim endless fields of grass and turn it into profit! Start small with your trusty scissors and backpack, then walk around cutting every patch you see. Sell your freshly cut grass for coins and upgrade your tools to cover more ground faster. Explore new areas, unlock better gear, and become the ultimate lawn-cutting pro! Grab your scissors and start mowing today!
