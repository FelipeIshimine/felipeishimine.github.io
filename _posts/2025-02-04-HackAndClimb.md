## Description

Physics-based precision platformer and the original game jam build that started it all. Made in under a week for **Pirate Jam 16** with the theme *"You are the weapon"* — the player character IS the projectile.

This jam version later grew into the full Steam release.

## Roles
Programmer, Game Designer, Level Designer, SFX Artist, VFX Artist
